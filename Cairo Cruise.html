Cairo Cruise

Program

Arrive in Cairo
• Once you have arrived in Cairo, transfer to your hotel.
• The rest of the day is free at leisure to explore the city.

Cairo - Train
• This morning, experience a full day tour visiting the Giza Pyramids, Papyrus institute, the Egyptian Museum, Perfume factory and shopping.
• In the evening, transfer to the train station for your overnight train to Aswan.
• Overnight on the sleeper train.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Aswan - Nile Cruise
• This morning, visit the Aswan High Dam. Afterwards, see the Unfinished Obelisks in Aswan's ancient granite quarries and view the Temple of Philae, dedicated to the ancient Egyptian goddess Isis.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

4 Nile Cruise - Kom Ombo - Edfu - Luxor
• Relax on board the boat as it sails towards Kom Ombo and upon arrival, view the the Temple of Kom Ombo, dedicated to the god Horus of the skies and Sobek, the crocodile god. Arrive at the Temple of Horus at Edfu, a sandstone structure completed in 57 BC by Cleopatra's father, Ptolemy XII. Re-embark and savour the scenes along the riverbanks, giving you an insight into the everyday life of Egyptians. Cross the Esna Lock, on the way to Luxor.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Luxor
• Arrive into Luxor and visit the Necropolis of Thebes before continuing to the Valley of the Kings, the site of nearly all the pharaohs' tombs. On the eastern side of Thebes is the magnificent Temple of Hatshepsut.Before lunch, you will visit Alabaster factory and Colossi of Memnon. Proceed to Luxor Temple and Karnak temple before return to your Cruise.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Luxor - Train
• Disembark and visit the Karnak Temple then proceed to Luxor Temple which a large Ancient Egyptian temple complex Transfer To Train Station for your Over Night Train To Cairo.
Meals: Breakfast

Cairo
• After breakfast On Board Of Train , Arrive Cairo, then full day tour Old Cairo and Khan El Khalli Bazaar Back To Hotel & Over Night .
Meals: Breakfast .

Depart Cairo
• After breakfast, enjoy your own activities at leisure till you will be picked up and transferred to the airport for your departing flight.
Meals: Breakfast
